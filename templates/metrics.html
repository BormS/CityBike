Duration and number of the trips per month

City Bike provides data of bicycles usage in New-York. The usage of the bikes and the duration of the trips in summer months is about twice higher than in winter ones.

Duration and number of the trips per weekday

The number of trips is rapidly falling for the weekend. Probably, the major part of the traffic falls on those who use bikes to go to work or school. Though, on weekend the duration of the trips is about 1.5 longer.

Duration and number of the trips by days

Overall, the trend of number of trips is increasing. Over 4 years the average number of trips has increased by about 250 trips per day (from 750 to 1000). Due to the Covid-19 pandemic and self-isolation, people started massively useing bicycles as substitute for public transport and as workout.

Number of bikes

To provide necessary number of bicyckes it is important to look back on the statistics and to see the number of bikes that were used during previous years. This understanding can help City Bike to avoid the lack of the bikes during pick hours/days/months andThe provide just enough of them in less popular seasons.
